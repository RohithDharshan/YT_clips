Features

Everything between raw footage and ready-to-post

One pipeline finds the moments; one editor makes them perfect. No timelines to fight, no exports to babysit.

AI highlight detection

Audio energy, scene motion, hooks and climax moments — every second is scored so the best 1% rises to the top.

Smart reframing

Shot-aware cropping locks onto the face or the action — and backs off to full frame when the moment is too wide to cut.

Word-perfect captions

Whisper word timestamps burned in with karaoke, bold or minimal styles — the active word pops, every time.

Built-in editor

Trim with draggable handles, restyle captions, switch framing, crop & zoom manually — then re-render just that clip.

Every format

9:16 for Shorts and Reels, 1:1 for feeds, 16:9 for YouTube — or any custom ratio, all rendered at full quality.

Virality analytics

Hook strength, climax, audio and visual scores per clip — know why a moment was chosen before you post it.

How it works

Three steps. Minutes, not hours.

Drop a link or a file

Paste a YouTube URL or upload MP4, MOV, MKV or WEBM. Pick duration, ratio, captions and framing.

The AI does the editing

Transcribe → analyze the subject → score every moment → render ranked, reframed, captioned clips.

Fine-tune & export

Adjust any clip in the studio — trim, styles, manual crop — and download one clip or the whole set as a ZIP.
