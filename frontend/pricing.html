      Questions

      What counts as "a video"?

      Every time you paste a YouTube link or upload a file and generate clips from it, that's one video against your monthly quota. Re-rendering or restyling clips from a video you already processed is always free and doesn't count again.

      What happens if I go over my source-length limit?

      ClipMind checks the video's length before transcribing it. If it's longer than your plan allows, you'll get a clear message and nothing is charged against your quota — just trim the video or upgrade.

      Can I cancel anytime?

      Yes — Pro is billed monthly (or annually at a discount) with no lock-in. Cancelling keeps your account on the Free plan going forward.

      Do I need a credit card for the Free plan?

      No. Sign in with your Google account and you're creating clips in under a minute.
